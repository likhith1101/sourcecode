Gaddam Likhitheshwar

Gaddam Likhitheshwar (born November 1, 2001) is an Indian. He is a software engineer trainee in Prodapt.

Early Life

Likhith was born in Hyderabad, Telangana on November 1, 2001. He is the son of Gaddam Ramulu and Vatnala Saraswathi. His ancestry includes Telugu. His father was a businessman and his mother is a homemaker.
